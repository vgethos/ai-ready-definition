Beneath the input is a **reCAPTCHA** widget with a checkbox, the label "I'm not a robot," the reCAPTCHA logo, and links to "Privacy" and "Terms."

### What the user can do
- **Email text input** — Enter an email address; placeholder shows "example@ethos.com"
- **reCAPTCHA checkbox** — Check "I'm not a robot" to complete the bot verification
- **"Log in" link** — Navigates existing account holders to a login flow
- **"Privacy" link** (within reCAPTCHA) — Opens Google's reCAPTCHA privacy policy
- **"Terms" link** (within reCAPTCHA) — Opens Google's reCAPTCHA terms of service
- **"Next" button** — Submits the email and advances to the next step (currently disabled until the form is valid)
- **Back arrow** — Returns to the previous screen in the funnel
