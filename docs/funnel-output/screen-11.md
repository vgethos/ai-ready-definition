## Screen 11

**URL:** https://app.ethos.com/q/mortgageDebtAmounts

### What the user sees
- **Header**: "ETHOS" logo centered at the top, back arrow (←) on the left, "NEED HELP? (415) 915-0665" on the right
- **Progress bar**: Thin green bar near the top indicating funnel progress
- **Question heading**: "About how much remaining debt/mortgage do you have?"
- **Field label**: "Debt/mortgage amount"
- **Current value display**: "$0" shown in large text
- **Slider**: A horizontal range slider positioned at the far left (minimum), spanning from "$0" to "$1M+"
- **Info callout**: A light blue/gray box with an info icon containing the text: "Getting an estimate of your debt helps us understand your coverage needs."

### What the user can do
- **Back arrow (←)**: Navigate to the previous screen
- **"NEED HELP? (415) 915-0665" link**: Tap to call support
- **Debt/mortgage amount slider**: Drag to select a dollar amount ranging from $0 to $1M+; currently set to $0
- **"Next" button**: Large black button at the bottom; submits the selected debt amount and advances to the next screen
