## Screen 21

**URL:** https://app.ethos.com/q/name

### What the user sees
- **Header:** "ETHOS" logo centered at the top; "NEED HELP? (415) 915-0665" in the top-right corner; back arrow in the top-left; teal progress bar near full completion beneath the header
- **Heading:** "What's your name?"
- **Subtext:** "This helps us verify your identity so you can **get your final rate**, not just an estimate." ("get your final rate" is a teal hyperlink)
- **Field label:** "Legal first name" with a pre-filled value of "joe" and a lock icon
- **Field label:** "Legal last name" with a pre-filled value of "pesci" and a lock icon
- **Security note:** Lock icon followed by "We use secure encryption to protect your data."
- **Consent copy:** "By clicking "Continue", I confirm that I am the applicant and that I have received, read, and agree to:" followed by a list of underlined links: "HIPAA Authorization", "E-Sign Consent", "Phone/SMS Consent", "Consumer Report Authorization", "Insurance Practices", "Replacement Notice", "Terms of Use", "Privacy Policy", and "MIB Pre-notice & Authorization"

### What the user can do
- **Legal first name** text input — enter or edit their legal first name (pre-filled with "joe")
- **Legal last name** text input — enter or edit their legal last name (pre-filled with "pesci")
- **"get your final rate"** link — navigates to more information about final rate vs. estimate
- **"HIPAA Authorization"** link — opens HIPAA Authorization document
- **"E-Sign Consent"** link — opens E-Sign Consent document
- **"Phone/SMS Consent"** link — opens Phone/SMS Consent document
- **"Consumer Report Authorization"** link — opens Consumer Report Authorization document
- **"Insurance Practices"** link — opens Insurance Practices document
- **"Replacement Notice"** link — opens Replacement Notice document
- **"Terms of Use"** link — opens Terms of Use document
- **"Privacy Policy"** link — opens Privacy Policy document
- **"MIB Pre-notice & Authorization"** link — opens MIB Pre-notice & Authorization document
- **"Continue"** button (dark/black, full-width) — submits the name and proceeds to the next step, implicitly accepting all listed consents
