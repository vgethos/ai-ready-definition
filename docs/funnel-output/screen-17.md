### What the user sees
The screen displays the **ETHOS** logo centered at the top, with a back arrow on the left and "NEED HELP? (415) 915-0665" in teal on the right. A teal progress bar spans roughly half the width of the screen beneath the header.

The main heading reads: **"Are you a citizen or permanent resident of the USA?"** in large, bold dark text.

Below the heading are three selectable options displayed as light gray bordered cards, each with left-aligned text:
1. **U.S. Citizen**
2. **Permanent Resident (Green Card)**
3. **None of the above**

### What the user can do
- **Back arrow (top left)** — navigates to the previous screen
- **"(415) 915-0665" link (top right)** — calls Ethos support
- **"U.S. Citizen" option card** — selects U.S. Citizen as citizenship status; advances the funnel
- **"Permanent Resident (Green Card)" option card** — selects Permanent Resident as citizenship status; advances the funnel
- **"None of the above" option card** — selects neither status; likely triggers a disqualification or alternative flow
