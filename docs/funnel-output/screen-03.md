## Screen 3

**URL:** https://app.ethos.com/q/goals

### What the user sees
- **Header:** "ETHOS" logo (top left) and "NEED HELP? (415) 915-0665" (top right)
- **Heading:** "Let's get started! What are your goals for life insurance?"
- **Subtext:** "Select all that apply."
- **Four selectable options**, each with an icon and label:
  1. "Protect my loved ones" (icon: two people)
  2. "Leave an inheritance" (icon: hand with coin)
  3. "Cover my funeral expenses" (icon: shield with checkmark)
  4. "I'm not sure" (icon: hand with question gesture)
- All options are unselected (empty radio/checkbox circles on the right)

### What the user can do
- **"Protect my loved ones"** — selectable option (checkbox/radio); choose this goal
- **"Leave an inheritance"** — selectable option; choose this goal
- **"Cover my funeral expenses"** — selectable option; choose this goal
- **"I'm not sure"** — selectable option; choose this if goal is unclear
- **"Next" button** (bottom, full-width, gray/disabled) — advances to the next step once at least one option is selected
- **Phone number "(415) 915-0665"** (top right) — tap to call for help
