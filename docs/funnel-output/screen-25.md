- **Apt/Suite input** — Enter optional apartment or suite number; labeled "Apt/Suite"
- **Next button** — Disabled/grayed out at the bottom; proceeds to the next step once address is filled in
- **Phone number link** — "(415) 915-0665" in the top-right; tapping calls customer support
