## Screen 9

**URL:** https://app.ethos.com/q/wills

### What the user sees
The Ethos logo appears centered at the top of the screen, with a back arrow on the left and "NEED HELP? (415) 915-0665" on the top right. A thin green progress bar spans the top, approximately one-third filled. The main heading reads: **"Do you have an Estate Plan or Will in place?"** Three answer options are displayed as full-width cards below the heading.

### What the user can do
- **Back arrow** (top left) — navigate to the previous screen
- **"(415) 915-0665"** (top right) — tap to call Ethos support
- **"Yes"** option card — select to indicate they have an Estate Plan or Will
- **"No"** option card — select to indicate they do not have one
- **"I'm not sure"** option card — select if they are uncertain
