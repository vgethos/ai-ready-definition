### What the user can do

- **"Check my price"** (green button, hero section) — primary CTA to start the insurance quote funnel
- **"Log in"** (top nav) — access existing account
- **"Term Life"**, **"Whole Life"**, **"Learn"**, **"About"** (top nav links) — navigate to product/info pages
- **"Get started"** (green button, "life insurance made easy" section) — secondary CTA into the funnel
- **App Store badge** — download the Ethos mobile app
- **FAQ accordion items** — expand/collapse individual FAQ answers
- **"Check my price"** (green button, bottom CTA section) — third entry point into the quote funnel
- **Footer links** — navigate to various informational and legal pages
