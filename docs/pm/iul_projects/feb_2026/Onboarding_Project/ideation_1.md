## What Stays True Across Both Flows

- The subject is always the family and what they could receive — never the user's personal wealth
- Cash value mechanics live only in footnotes
- Each screen adds exactly one idea
- The floor protection is always scoped to market dips specifically — never framed as an absolute guarantee
- Access is framed as a flexibility bonus subordinate to the inheritance story, not a parallel benefit
- Compliance language uses "could" throughout — never "will"
