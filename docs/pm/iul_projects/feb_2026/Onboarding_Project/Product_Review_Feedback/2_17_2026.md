# IUL Onboarding Meeting Notes

---

## Key Takeaways

### Language & Terminology
- **"Returns" is forbidden.** Replace with "payout" (e.g., "guaranteed payout" not "guaranteed returns").
- **Define "wealth" consistently:** In this context, wealth = growth in the death benefit for loved ones — it is NOT money the policyholder uses during their lifetime. Avoid saying "during your life" when referring to wealth.
- **Do not say** the whole death benefit grows with the market — only the cash value does.
- **Do not imply** the entire death benefit grows at 8.5% — only cash value grows at that rate.
- Replace "accessing growth" language with "you get a chunk of it" — the focus is on access, not growth.
- Term life option should be framed as: "A fixed amount that loved ones will get" (not low/high growth returns).

### What You CAN Say
- A portion of the policy (cash value) grows with the market.
- Loved ones could receive 3–5X what the policyholder puts in.
- The total policy value / death benefit is not negatively affected when the market crashes.
- Cash value has a 0% floor — protected from market losses.
- "Wealth" = death benefit growth; policyholders can access (borrow) a part of it.

### Core Design Principles
- Avoid trade-off questions — don't force users to choose between features they get with both options.
- The cash value screen must be **additive and exciting**, not competing with or confusing the death benefit story.
- Both the growth story AND the safety story need to be told — communicating only "it won't go down" misses the growth narrative.

## Suggestions & Ideas

### Onboarding Variants
- **Variant 1:** "Wealth" = growth on the death benefit only.
- **Variant 2:** "Wealth" = what the policyholder can use (cash value access).

### Growth/Safety Screen Options (3 Approaches to Test)
1. **Two Simplified Messages**
   - Message A: Safety focus — *"Your IUL growth is safe; your gains don't dip even if the market crashes."*
   - Message B: Explicit market upside + no downside connection.
2. **Combined Screen** — Show both 3–4X growth AND loss protection on a single screen.
3. **Two Light Screens in a Row** — Keep growth and protection as separate screens rather than forcing a single question.

### Cash Value / Access Screen Ideas
1. **Additional benefit discovery** — After establishing the death benefit story, introduce cash value as "there's more to your policy."
2. **"Did you know?" framing** — *"Your policy also builds cash value you can access during your lifetime."* Positions it as a bonus, not a trade-off.
3. **Sequential reveal** — Screen 1: death benefit growth & protection. Screen 2: *"Plus, you're also building wealth you can use."*
4. **Life scenario tie-in** — *"Want to access funds for emergencies or opportunities while you're alive?"*
5. **Simple interest check** — *"Interested in learning how you can access your policy's value during your lifetime?"* Only users who say yes receive deeper cash value education.
6. **Retirement income angle** — *"Do you want retirement income?"* Follow-up: *"The more you put in, the more you can use."*

### Terminology Swap Suggestions
| Avoid | Use Instead |
|---|---|
| "Guaranteed returns" | "Guaranteed payout" |
| "Low growth guaranteed returns" | "A fixed amount your loved ones will receive" |
| "Accessing the growth" | "You can borrow/access a portion of it" |
| "Wealth during your life" | "Wealth your loved ones receive" / "funds you can access" |
