### Messaging & Copy Changes

- [ ] **Cash value question:** Change from "How do you want to use the wealth you grow?" to "What do you want to use it for?"

- [ ] **Rename:** "Kids Inheritance" to "Inheritance for my loved ones" (user may not have kids at this point).

### Compliance-Driven Changes

- [ ] Do NOT say "Your death benefit grows with the market." Most of the death benefit does not actually grow with the market.
- [ ] Separate death benefit messaging from cash value messaging into distinct screens:
  - **Screen 1 (Death Benefit):** "With IUL, your family gets 4-5X what you put in." No market language.
  - **Screen 2 (Cash Value):** "Your cash value grows with the market and never goes down." Introduce via investment preference question.
- [ ] Do not associate wealth building with the death benefit. Make it clear that market-linked growth refers to cash value only.
- [ ] "IULs grow wealth with the market" will be interpreted by compliance as a cash value statement. Do not use it in a death benefit context.

### Visual Design

- [ ] Align CTA button color with Calvin. Dark black CTA on dark background is too easy to miss and doesn't feel elevated.
- [ ] Consider using Clover CTA or other alternatives currently being tested.

### Policy Configuration

- [ ] Team is orienting toward additive death benefit (not level DB) up to age 65.
- [ ] Cash value not used goes to family in addition to face value.

## Agreed Flow Structure

The team aligned on this sequence for the IUL onboarding funnel:

| Step | Screen | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | **Death Benefit Education** | "Your family gets 4-5X what you put in." Explain that IUL grows and the family gets it. No market language here. |
| 2 | **Investment Preference Question** | Ask risk tolerance. Frame as "slow growth guaranteed returns" vs "higher growth with no downside." Introduce guaranteed vs. non-guaranteed concepts. |
| 3 | **Family Protection Questions** | "Who depends on you financially?" + "How many children?" + What they want to leave for family. Then explain the money growing goes to their family. |
| 4 | **Cash Value Usage Question** | "What do you want to use it for?" Options: Retirement income, Emergencies, Milestones. Explain borrowing mechanics: pay it back it stays, don't pay it back family gets the rest. |
| 5 | **Transition to PII** | Chapter screen before collecting personal information. |

## Brainstorming & Ideas to Explore

Ideas raised during discussion that need further exploration or validation.

### Risk Tolerance Question Design

- Add a risk tolerance / investment preference question between screens 2-3 to set up the 0% floor message.
- Frame options to avoid making IUL sound "too good to be true":
  - "Slow growth, guaranteed returns" (examples: bonds)
  - "Higher growth with no downside" (examples: market-linked, protected from dips)
- For users who select guaranteed returns, consider recommending term life instead: "If you want complete certainty for 30 years, go get that, because whole life is terrible."

### Cash Value Playback Concept

- After user selects their use case, play back the answer with borrowing mechanics: "You borrow, put it back, it's yours; don't put it back, your family gets the rest."
- This could introduce guaranteed vs. non-guaranteed concepts earlier in the flow.

### Death Benefit Messaging Angles

- "From day one protection" or "no matter when you pass" as alternative angles.
- Acceptable framing: "Your loved ones will get 3 to 4x what you put in" (or "could get").
- Consider: "Whatever happens, they get it" for the death benefit message.

### Tone Consistency

- Screens 2-4 are informational/educational, but screen 5 shifts to call-to-action. Maintain consistent informative tone throughout.
- Cash value doesn't need to be the heading. It can be a sub-bullet or footnote.
- IUL has volatility and uncertainty, which should be acknowledged somewhere.

### Bottom-of-Funnel Transparency

- How do we make it clear during onboarding that the difference between guaranteed and non-guaranteed is the stock market?
- Goal: don't surprise people later in the funnel with non-guaranteed elements.
- Open question: Dig deeper into why projections are not guaranteed after year 20.

### Screen Consolidation Messaging

- Combined message idea: "IUL grows wealth with the market and saves from the dip."
- Note: This phrasing may have compliance issues if not clearly tied to cash value.

## Compliance Guardrails (Reference)

Key compliance constraints raised by Ash and the team:

| CAN Say | CANNOT Say |
|---------|------------|
| "Your loved ones could get 3 to 4x what you put in" | "Your death benefit grows with the market" |
| "Your family could get 4-5X what you put in" | "IULs grow wealth with the market" in a death benefit context |
| "Your cash value grows with the market" (clearly labeled as cash value) | "Set for life" (implies never working again) |
| "Cash value is protected from market losses" (with disclaimers) | Associating wealth building language with death benefit |
| Lifelong protection based upon premium payments above certain thresholds (disclaimer) | Borrowing framed as a "both" story (it's one or the other) |
