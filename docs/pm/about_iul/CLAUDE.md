## Compliance Guardrails

These apply to all IUL work across this repo:

**Forbidden language**
- "Returns" → use "payout" (e.g. "guaranteed payout" not "guaranteed returns")
- "Your death benefit grows with the market" — only cash value does
- "IULs grow wealth with the market" in a death benefit context
- "Set for life" — implies never working again
- Associating wealth-building language with the death benefit

**Required distinctions**
- Cash value grows with the market (cap 8.5%, floor 0%) — death benefit does not
- "Wealth" in the death benefit context = growth that goes to loved ones, NOT money the policyholder uses during their lifetime
- Loved ones *could* receive 3–5× what the policyholder puts in — use "could" or "typically"
- Borrowing from the policy is one or the other: pay it back (it stays), don't pay it back (family gets it) — not a "both" story
