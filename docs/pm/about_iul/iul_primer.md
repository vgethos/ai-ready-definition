# Q4 '25: IUL Primer

## Jobs to Be Done

1. **Grow my wealth (via investing)**
   - I get more money out than I put in.
   - Reasons include:
     - **Leaving a legacy**
     - **Diversify financial portfolio**
       - Add non-correlated asset that doesn't move with stock market but captures some upside
   - How:
     - **Achieve higher growth potential with downside protection**
       - Want ~6-7% historical returns while avoiding market losses (0% floor)

2. **Protect family with permanent death benefit**
   - Lifelong coverage that doesn't expire like term insurance

3. **Access while alive**
   - **Build supplemental retirement income**
     - Primary driver for IUL customers who want tax-advantaged income in retirement years
   - **Access emergency funds tax-free**
     - Ability to take loans

4. **Maximize tax advantages / tax-advantaged access while alive**
   - Tax-deferred growth, tax-free death benefit, and tax-free access to cash value via loans

---

## Marketing Messages

1. **Protection That Grows With You — Death Benefit + Cash Value**
   - Emphasize dual-purpose nature (addresses both protection and growth goals)

2. **Tax-Advantaged Growth For Retirement**
   - Tax-deferred accumulation
   - Tax-free loans for retirement income
   - Must include: "Tax-free assumes policy doesn't lapse. Consult your tax advisor."

3. **Capture Market Upside With Zero Downside Risk**
   - Highlight 0% floor protection with growth potential averaging 5.56%

4. **Instant Approval, No Medical Exam Required**
   - Must include proximity disclosure: "Approval based on health questions in application"
   - Emphasize speed/convenience vs traditional IUL policies

5. **Flexibility When Life Changes**
   - Pay more when you can, less when needed
   - Access your money for opportunities or emergencies
   - Adjustable coverage amounts
